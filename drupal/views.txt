Advanced help
drupal_7_views_cookbook.pdf
Recall and familiarize with all options, settings available in Views UI first.

 

handler
-------
a class that takes care of field display, sorting, filtering, contextual
filtering, or relationships.

plugin
------
a class related to the overall display of the View, and other classes that
take care of the basic functions of Views.
